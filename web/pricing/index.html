Frequently Asked Questions

When do tradies get charged?

Tradies are only charged the lead fee when they actively accept a job invite and unlock the homeowner's contact information. You will never be charged just for receiving a lead request.

Are there card processing fees?

Standard Stripe card processing fees apply to escrow transactions. This is typically 1.75% + 30¢ for domestic cards, which is clearly outlined before any payment is made.
