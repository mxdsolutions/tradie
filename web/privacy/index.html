Privacy Policy

Last updated: Feb 15, 2026

At BasePro, your project integrity and data privacy are our highest priorities. This policy outlines how we handle your information.

1. Data Collection

We collect essential information to facilitate secure construction payments, including identity verification for Stripe and project evidence (photos/videos).

2. Security

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We leverage Supabase and Stripe's world-class infrastructure to ensure your data is never compromised.

3. Third Parties

We do not sell your data. We only share information with essential service providers like Stripe for payments and AWS for evidence storage.
